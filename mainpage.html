Carnegie Mellon TechBridge Coding Boot Camp

History

Carnegie Mellon University established a coding boot camp program through a partnership with TechBridge in 2024. The first full time cohort began in September of that year with daily online classes. The program was expanded in February 2025 with another full time cohort and a new part time cohort. The program is being expanded further, with classes that concentrate on a single programming language, beginning in the late summer of 2025.

This web site relates stories and experiences of some of the students. Thank you for visiting, and please explore these pages to learn more about them.
